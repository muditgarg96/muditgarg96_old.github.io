About me


I was born in Milan, Italy, where I also attended highschool. I moved to Zürich in Switzerland in order to study physics at ETH, and have lived here since. I enjoy the German and English languages and their literature, D&D, prog rock and surreal humor. The seed of my love of physics and science was planted early, by the sci-fi stories of I. Aasimov, R. Bradbury, A. Huxley, F. Herbert and P. K. Dick, among many other.

I am also keen mountaineer and rock-climber, having completed several ascents in the alpine region. I am happy to share some below.
